How It Works

Simple 3-step process to get food from donors to those who need it most

1. Donate

Have surplus food? List it on netfoodia and help reduce waste while feeding those in need.

2. Volunteer Picks Up

Our dedicated volunteers collect the food from your location at a convenient time.

3. Delivered to Charity

Food is delivered to local charities and organizations that distribute it to those who need it most.
